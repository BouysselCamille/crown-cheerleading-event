Additional Rules

Prep teams will follow the relevant IASF rules with the following additions:

  • No tosses permitted in these divisions — Clarification: This includes 'Sponge' (also known as load in or Squish) tosses. Clarification: All waist level cradles are illegal.
  • In the All Star Prep levelling system the 1st number refers to the building level, the 2nd to the tumbling level. Example: All Star Prep Level 2.1 follows level 2 building rules and level 1 tumbling rules.

Additional Rules
  • Divisions may be split into Small vs Large, or All Girl vs Coed, provided this leaves a minimum of 3 teams per division. The split point for size is determined based on the number of registered athletes, not on the day of the event. If a team registers in large but performs with fewer athletes than expected, they will not be moved to a different division. These splits remain at the discretion of the organizing team.

Additional Rules
  • 90% of the team must be currently enrolled students at the mentioned university.
  • 10% of the team may be alumni, provided they graduated from that university within the last 4 years.
  • Proof of university eligibility is required: student ID and/or proof of graduation within 4 years preceding the event date.
  • University teams are scored under the University rubric (See United Scoring › United Kingdom › University).
